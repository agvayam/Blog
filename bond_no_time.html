"No Time to Die" Committed the Worst James Bond Crime!

  • August 16, 2022

SPOILER ALERT!

Daniel Craig is retiring as James Bond. This doesn’t mean ‘No Time to Die’ had the right to kill him. It is a crime! James Bond was probably 50. They could have at least made him the director of MI6. I am pissed.

Well, it is what it is. I have no clue what’s going on in the heads of the Neal Purvis, Robert Wade and the other writers. But based on the movie, I think they have left avenues open for the future of Bond. Here is what I predict future Bond movies are going to look like.

They introduced a new 007 agent - agent Nomi. Are they planning to continue the Bond franchise with her? In ‘No Time to Die’, Nomi gains respect for Bond and he mentored her till some extent. Here is what I imagine the future will look like – Nomi will bring diversity to the James Bond series and make the franchise more inclusive. There was a lot of debate about how Bond treated women. I think Nomi as a character would make up for the sins Bond’s character committed in the 20th century.

Bond also has a daughter – Mathilda Swann. Oh, she is adorable. James Bond gave me another reason to become like him haha. Here is a cool plot – Mathilda will become Mathilda Swann Bond. And she will go to the Island where Bond was killed. There, she will realize that Bond is alive and is living in isolation and make it her goal to get him back.

And let us make Mathilda a scientist. Let us make Mathilda driven by the purpose of eliminating project Heracles (the virus that led to Bond killing himself). In the end, Bond has a reunion with Mathilda and his wife – Madeline. And there is the happy ending. Daniel Craig as Bond won’t have to perform any stunts, and maybe MGM would be able to convince him to come back.

I am writing an email to the writers of Bond. If this works out, I’ll leave Computer Science and become a filmmaker haha. My mom will be so mad at me.
